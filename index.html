My name is David Kim.


I am an amateur photography hobbyist based in New Jersey, USA.


I'm in love with capturing the beauty in the mundane. Too often do we rush about our daily lives and miss the beauty in every moment.


I look to document life as is, pursuing beauty in both the highest of highs and the lowest of lows.


I wish to show the world that even the most mundane moments are beautiful.


My goal is to candidly display the world as it is, aesthetically pleasing in every moment.
